Publications

Mouse sensorimotor cortex reflects complex kinematic details during reaching and grasping

Using two-photon calcium imaging and linear decoding models, this work examined kinematic encoding across primary motor and somatosensory cortex during a reach-to-grasp task; revealing that both areas richly represent fine-grained joint angle details, while differing in the timing of higher-level, target-specific signals.

Neural activity profiles reveal overlapping, intermingled subpopulations spanning area borders in mouse sensorimotor cortex

Using feature engineering and Gaussian mixture models, this work examined single neuron tuning properties across sensorimotor cortex; discovering distinct subpopulations with characteristic activity patterns that span and intermingle across anatomical areas.
